The shift

Agents don't just think — they act

AI agents now operate across your real systems:

Post messages to channels and timelines Publish content to external platforms Modify systems, databases, and repositories Trigger workflows with real business impact

Most systems ensure agents run.
Nothing ensures they act correctly for your business.

The problem

Technically safe doesn't mean correct

An agent can take a valid action that has the wrong business impact.

Send the wrong message to the right channel

Take valid actions with bad business consequences

Execute workflows that are logically incorrect

These are not technical failures. They are business failures.

How it works

Policy-driven execution

01

Agent proposes

An agent attempts an action — post a message, open a PR, publish content.

02

Surfit classifies

The action is evaluated against your policies and assigned a Wave level (1–5).

03

Action is routed

Low risk executes automatically. Medium is logged. High requires checks. Critical is escalated.

04

Everything is recorded

Every action gets a full execution log — what happened, when, why, and who decided.
