1. Service Description

Surfit provides a decision layer for AI agent actions ("Service"). The Service evaluates agent-proposed actions in business context, classifies risk using a Wave scoring system (1-5), and either executes actions automatically or holds them for human review. The Service includes credential management, audit logging, compliance reporting, and related dashboards.
